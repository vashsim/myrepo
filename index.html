Advantages of booking through OffenOffice

You can simply check-in and start working!

If you want to combine work and vacation, it's important that everything works perfectly. You don't need to worry whether your rented space is suitably equipped for your office-needs or not. We grade all our accomodations as per the OffenOffice score!

You don't have to worry about transactions or cancellations!

All accommodations and offices on our website are graded and certified by us. Bookings and payments are secured through our portal. Your money is kept safely with us until the day of arrival, so in case of cancellation (e.g. by Corona) you'll get your money back quickly!

You don't have to worry about any enquiries!

Get more involved in your lifestyle persuits, because we will handle all the customer enquiries for you. If you wish to start hosting, please send us an inquiry via our requirements form & we will get back to you within 24 hours. We are there for you 7 days a week!
