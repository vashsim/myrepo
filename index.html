Advantages of booking through OffenOffice

Accomodations are graded as per the OffenOffice score.

If you want to combine work and vacation, it is important that everything works perfectly. That is why our accommodations are are not only selected according to how beautiful the locations are, but also graded as per the ease of working onsite, privacy & available facilities (by the "OffenOffice Score").

We care for your security & peace of mind.

All accommodations and workplaces you find on our website have been certified by us. Bookings and payments are secured by us. Your money is kept safe by us until the day of arrival, so in case of cancellation (e.g. by Corona, other unforseeable circumstances) you will get your money back quickly.

We are there for you. 24 hours a day, 7 days a week!

We are more than happy to advise you personally and find your individual dream office together with you. Please send us an inquiry via our contact form and we will get back to you within 24 hours - even on weekends, because we are there for you 7 days a week.
