Our Committment

To support the local start-up culture and remote workers.

Being a startup, we understand what it is to be a startup! We’ll help you find affordable workspaces, such as rooms, garages, offices, co-working spaces and pre-owned office furniture. We also believe that Home-office does not necessarily mean 'work from home'. We want to facilitate remote workers to work from anywhere in the world!

  • Learn more

To reduce the painful hours of commuting for work.

Whether your company is a startup with a small, dedicated team or a large, established corporation, where you're headquartered doesn't have to limit where your company operates. With satellite offices, we aim to provide the right balance between your costs and business prospects, prioritizing the convenience of your employees.

  • Learn more

To promote re-use of pre-owned office furniture.

It is possible to re-use office furniture and equipments and reduce consumerism. By re-selling or giving away, we minimise the waste produced in your clearance and maximise the materials that can be reused. We are striving for 100% re-use of office furniture and equipments.
